Monika Spiced Grilled Prawns

A house favourite ‚Äî succulent king prawns marinated in our signature West African spice blend, chargrilled to smoky perfection and finished with a zesty Monika sauce.

Download Recipe (PDF)
What You'll Need

Ingredients

  • 500g raw king prawns, shell-on
  • 2 tbsp Monika spice blend (paprika, cayenne, garlic powder, onion powder, dried thyme)
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 scotch bonnet pepper, finely diced
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • Fresh coriander and lime wedges, for serving

Method

  1. Combine the spice blend, garlic, scotch bonnet, lime juice, and olive oil. Toss the prawns in the marinade, cover, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat a charcoal grill or griddle pan to high heat until smoking.
  3. Grill the prawns for 2‚Äì3 minutes per side until charred and cooked through.
  4. Serve immediately with fresh coriander, lime wedges, and our Monika dipping sauce.
